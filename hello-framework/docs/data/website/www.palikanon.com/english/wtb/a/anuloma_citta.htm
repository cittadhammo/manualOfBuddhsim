• anuloma-citta

'adaptation-moment of consciousness',

denotes the third of the 4 moments of impulsion (javana, q.v.) flashing up immediately before either reaching the absorptions (jhāna, q.v.) or the supermundane paths (s. ariya-puggala).

These 4 moments of impulsion are:

  • preparation (parikamma),
  • access (upacāra),
  • adaptation (anuloma)
  • maturity (gotrabhū) moments.

For further details, s. javana, gotrabhū.
